# Privacy Policy for Simple Games

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Simple Games mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect any personal data or information from users of this App.**

The App is designed to be completely privacy-friendly:
- No personal information is collected
- No usage data is tracked
- No analytics are implemented
- No user accounts are required
- No internet connection is needed to play

## Third-Party Services

**We do not use any third-party services** that collect, monitor, or analyze user data.

Specifically:
- No advertising networks
- No analytics services
- No crash reporting services
- No social media integrations

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data** because we do not collect any data in the first place.

## Data Storage

**No data is stored** on our servers or any third-party servers. The App operates entirely offline and locally on your device. Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted anywhere.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Advertising

**The App contains no advertisements** and never will. We do not display ads, and we do not work with advertising networks.

## Internet Connectivity

**The App does not require internet connectivity** and does not transmit any data over the network. The App works completely offline.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository:

**GitHub Issues:** https://github.com/cpuchip/simple-games-public/issues

## Summary

To summarize our privacy practices:
- ✅ **Zero data collection**
- ✅ **No tracking or analytics**
- ✅ **No advertisements**
- ✅ **No internet connection required**
- ✅ **No third-party services**
- ✅ **Completely offline gameplay**
- ✅ **Safe for all ages**

Your privacy is important to us. This App is designed to be a simple, fun way to pass the time without any privacy concerns whatsoever.
